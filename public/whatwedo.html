WHAT WE DO

We know that a lot of homeless people face hardship in fighting the chilling winters. Through our determined effort, we help them to fight winters. We collect the clothes and other useful goods from people of university and distribute among the needed ones.

OUR MISSION AND VISSION

OUR MISSION

Building a large and strong network of university students and teachers, which will work to help those, who are in need during winter and hence buidling a better society.

OUR VISION

We Imagine a prosporous world in which societies are forever committed to caring for and valuing those , whoa are not as wealthy as they are, for a benefit of homeless people.
