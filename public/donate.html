Donate for a better cause

Due to variety of reasons such as lack of affordable housing based on rent cost rising above incomes, the level of homelessness has become a worrisome factor in the UK. There is a large number of people 'sleeping rough'. According to the charity Shelter, there are approximately 254,414 homeless people in England in 2016. The level of homelessness has overwhelmed the local councils in their efforts to address a rapidly growing phenomenon. And by this initiative we want to do our bit in Bristol.

Donate to Winter Hug and support our work to help vulnerable and less fortunate population of Bristol to fight winters. Your contributions will make a real difference and help us to provide warm hug to this population through donation of warm garments.

We have our donation boxes, that are available at various places near University of Bristol area. You can directly contribute there or can make your donations now. Donation boxes are available in following locations:

  • 1. Wills Memorial Building- main entrance.
  • 2. Merchant Venture Building- main entrance.
  • 3. Richmond Building- main entrance.
  • 4. Arts and Social Library- near cafeteria.
  • 5. Queens Buiding- main entrance and outside library.
  • 6. All Unite Student Residents.
