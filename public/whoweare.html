WHO WE ARE

We are a nonprofit organization which is run by some students of university of bristol. We are making our small contribution to make our society happier and prosperous.

KNOW MORE ABOUT US

Winter Hug is a young organization which helps homeless people during the winters by distributing helpful goods. We are funded and volunteered by people like you. We operate independently near the universities. No situation is beyond hope.

Winter Hug was incorporated on February 2017, with the help of some really good and positive students of university of bristol. Our desire was that nobody around university areas should sleep in chilling winter without enough warm clothes.
